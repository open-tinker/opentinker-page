Welcome to OpenTinker

December 25, 2025   |   Siqi Zhu

We are excited to introduce OpenTinker, an open-source platform designed to democratize Agentic Reinforcement Learning (RL) as a Service.

Why OpenTinker?

Reinforcement Learning, particularly with large language models (LLMs), has become increasingly complex. It often requires massive GPU resources and intricate setups that are out of reach for many researchers and developers.

OpenTinker aims to solve this by providing:

  • Disaggregated Architecture: Separate programming from execution. Run your client code anywhere, even on a laptop, while the heavy lifting happens on a dedicated cluster.
  • Unified Interface: Consistent APIs for both training and inference.
  • Extensible Design: Easily plug in new environments and agent loops.

Get Involved

OpenTinker is open source! Check out our GitHub repository to get started. Whether you want to contribute code, report bugs, or just try it out, we welcome your involvement.

Stay tuned for more updates, tutorials, and deep dives into the technology behind OpenTinker.
